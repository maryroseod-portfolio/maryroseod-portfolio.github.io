  • Social Media - Twitter & Facebook

    These are Facebook and Twitter posts I made for Pitt IT and The Pitt News. As Managing Editor of The Pitt News, I was often tasked to live-tweet various events. I introduced this real-time Twitter coverage to The Pitt News after shadowing journalists at 90.5 WESA (Pittsburgh’s NPR affiliate) who used social media to cover protests throughout Fall 2020. I thought this would be a great addition to The Pitt News’s coverage and a way to provide real-time and engaging digital journalism to our 20k Twitter followers instead of waiting until the next day for a full article to run on the website. For each of these Twitter threads, a reporter would text or Slack message me videos, images, and descriptions of what was happening at an event. I then used this information to post tweets in real time from my apartment as each event was happening. These Twitter threads had a high level of engagement compared to other posts on The Pitt News twitter account.
