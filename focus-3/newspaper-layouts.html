  • The Pitt News

    I was the layout editor for The Pitt News during the summer and early fall of 2020. I used Adobe InDesign to create three special print editions of the newspaper: New Student Guide (July 2020), Welcome Back Edition (August 2020), and First Day of Class Edition (August 2020). As Managing Editor during the 2020-2021 school year, I worked on the cover spread for our print edition after President Biden won the 2020 presidential election. This cover placed first in the Layout and Design category at the 2021 Student Keystone Media Awards (Division I).
