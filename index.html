Hello! My name is Mary Rose O’Donnell. I am a writer, technology-lover, and Pittsburgh transplant from West Chester, PA. I graduated from the University of Pittsburgh’s School of Computing and Information in December 2021 with a B.S. in Information Science and Digital Narrative & Interactive Design (DNID) with a concentration in nonfiction writing. I am looking to pursue a career path where I can combine my tech knowledge and skills with my love of writing. I created this website using HTML, CSS, Javascript, and the web development knowledge I acquired through my Information Science coursework. This website serves as an online portfolio for my work as a communications specialist, social media content creator, and journalist. My contact information and more about me can be found on my resume and LinkedIn profile.
