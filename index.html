About

Hi there! My name is Mary Rose O’Donnell and I’m an up-and-coming journalist from West Chester, PA. I am a senior at the University of Pittsburgh majoring in Information Science and Digital Narrative & Interactive Design and will graduate in May 2021. I am the Managing Editor for The Pitt News for the 2020-2021 school year and was previously a Newsroom Intern for 90.5 WESA. I am currently looking for jobs in journalism, media, marketing and communications.
